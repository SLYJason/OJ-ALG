# 4: Median of Two Sorted Arrays

### Solution 1: Two Pointers
**Time Complexity:**: `O(M + N)`, `M` and `N` is the size of `nums1` and `nums2` respectively.

**Space Complexity:**: `O(M + N)`, `M` and `N` is the size of `nums1` and `nums2` respectively.
